Keith Andres Bike Park

Andres Bike Park is located at Keith Andres Park, a 25 acre village park, in Carpentersville, IL and is built as a partnership between CAMBr and the Village of Carpentersville. Trails are currently under development and include a skills development area (currently open), flow trails, downhill gravity lines, hiking trails, and will also include a pump track, dirt jumps, and more fun for mountain and BMX bikes.

Big fun bike parks like this are expensive to build and maintain. Would you consider donating a dollar to build a foot of trail or sponsor a feature at Andres Bike Park?

For more park updates or to find out when you can help us build, please visit our Facebook page at http://www.facebook.com/andresbikepark or our official site for plans and more at http://www.andrespark.org.
